    2014 Drivers Who Have Left F1

    Jules Bianchi has been in a coma since Japanese Gran Prix in 2014.
    Max Chilton is racing with Nissan Motors­ports in the FIA World Endurance Champi­onship.
    Giedo van der Garde had a contract with Sauber for 2015, but following a legal fight is not expected to race. Unknown what he is doing next.
    Kamui Kobaya­shi is racing in Japanese Super Formula series.
    André Lotterer is racing with Audi Sport Team Joest in the FIA World Endurance Champi­onship.
    Adrian Sutil claimed to have had a contract with Sauber for 2015, but was not included in their driver lineup when announced.
