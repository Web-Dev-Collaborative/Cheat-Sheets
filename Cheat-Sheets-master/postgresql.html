Console

Commands

  • Show roles: \du
  • Show tables: \dt
  • Show databases: \l
  • Connect to a database: \c <database>
  • Show columns of a table: \d <table> or \d+ <table>
  • Quit: \q

Creating database

 $ createdb databasename
